Why work with us

WE ARE A COMMUNITY-BASED PRACTICE, WITH A FOCUS ON PROVIDING A STRESS FREE ENVIRONMENT FOR YOUR PET TO RECEIVE EXCEPTIONAL CARE. WE BUILD LONG-LASTING RELATIONSHIPS WITH OUR CLIENTS BY PROVIDING AN UNPARALLELED LEVEL OF PERSONAL SERVICE AND COMMUNICATION.

We endeavour to deliver world-class, up-to-date veterinary medicine with an unparalleled level of personal service and communication. We recognize the value of holistic regimens, which we often include in our treatment profiles. Our vets also have a close working relationship with Cape Town’s best veterinary specialists, meaning that you can have the comfort of knowing that your pets are receiving the best treatment available.

We’re practical and realistic, encouraging an open discussion about the treatment options for your pet – aware that personal, sentimental and financial considerations all have a role to play in the decisions you make to fulfil your needs and those of your pet.

All about cats

We know that bringing your cat to the vet can be one of the most stressful events in a cat’s life. Since we’re all about reducing stress, it makes sense that we have committed to being a Cat Friendly Clinic, and are recognised by the International Society of Feline Medicine. (We might be a little bit proud to be the first in South Africa to achieve this!) We have paid attention to the unique needs of cats in every area of our practice, from the waiting area to the consulting rooms to the hospital. Every member of staff has regular training to enhance our awareness of cats, and the ways in which we can make the visit easier for you and your cat.

Have a look at our CAT FRIENDLY resources, or talk to us about how we can help.
